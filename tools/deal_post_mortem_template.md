# Deal Post-Mortem Template
*48-hour structured review for every late-stage loss (Stage 3+)*

**Purpose:** Close the feedback loop between deal outcomes and upstream process. Without post-mortems, the same mistakes recur quarter after quarter. This template is designed to be completed in 15 minutes within 2 business days of the loss.

**When to use:** Every deal lost at Stage 3 (Technical Validation) or later. These are the most expensive losses — they consumed the most selling time, champion relationship, and organizational attention.

## Quarterly Aggregation

*This template feeds the quarterly closed-lost review. At end of quarter:*

1. Pull all post-mortems from the quarter
2. Categorize by root cause — what's the distribution?
3. Identify the top 2-3 patterns
4. For each pattern, define a systemic fix
5. Compare root cause distribution to prior quarter — are the same problems recurring?

**Capsule reference distribution (1,048 closed-lost deals):**
- 35% Unqualified → Tenet 4 (qualification)
- 25% Ghosting → Tenet 3 (buyer journey)
- 15% Bad Timing → Tenet 6 (targeting)
- 10% Wrong POC → Tenet 4 (discovery)
- 8% Pricing → Tenet 8 (pricing)
- 4% Competitor → Tenet 2 (positioning)

*If your distribution looks similar, your biggest leverage point is qualification. If it looks different, the distribution itself tells you where to focus.*
